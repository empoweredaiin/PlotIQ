# PlotIQ — Product, Brand & Systems Brief

## Overview

PlotIQ is building a regulatory operating system and intelligence infrastructure for urban development.

The company’s core IP is:

* encoded regulations
* computable entitlement logic
* regulatory interpretation systems
* feasibility intelligence
* development intelligence
* market intelligence infrastructure

PlotIQ begins with redevelopment entitlement understanding for housing societies, but evolves into a broader intelligence layer spanning:

* DCPR
* MIDC
* MHADA
* CIDCO
* redevelopment
* feasibility
* approvals
* incentives
* urban economics
* AEC workflows

The long-term vision is not a redevelopment app.

It is:

> a computational regulatory intelligence system for urban development.
