# PlotIQ — Master Branding & Positioning Brief

### Foundational Strategic Document

---

# 1. Introduction

PlotIQ is building a regulatory operating system and intelligence infrastructure for urban development.

The company’s core capability is not merely redevelopment calculation.

Its true capability is:

> encoding urban regulation into structured computational intelligence.

PlotIQ converts:

* regulations
* incentives
* approvals
* entitlement systems
* feasibility logic
* development constraints

into:

* interpretable intelligence
* operational assessments
* defensible outputs
* structured decision-making systems

The company begins with redevelopment intelligence for housing societies, but the long-term platform extends across:

* DCPR
* MIDC
* MHADA
* CIDCO
* redevelopment feasibility
* urban planning intelligence
* market intelligence
* AEC operational systems
* regulatory computation infrastructure

# 13. Information Architecture Philosophy

The current system exposes too much complexity directly.

The future system should:

* progressively reveal intelligence
* separate user layers
* compress computational complexity
* prioritize interpretation over calculation

The ideal information order is:

1. Context
2. Key assessment
3. Entitlement summary
4. Interpretation
5. Supporting logic
6. Clause references
7. Assumptions
8. Advanced computation

The user should first understand:

> “What does this mean?”

before seeing:

> “How was this computed?”
